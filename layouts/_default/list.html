About me

I'm a London based software developer, passionate about building and improving the web. For the last 5 years I've been a programmer at Basecamp, working across all their products including Basecamp 3, Highrise, and many more.

Before that I was a partner at Go Free Range, a small collective of talented, like-minded developers. While there we got FutureLearn off the ground, built the "government" part of GOV.UK (replacing all UK government departmental websites), and a number of smaller pieces of work for clients such as MUBI, O2 and others.
